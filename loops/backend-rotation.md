# Backend Rotation Map

Rotate one section per loop session so the whole platform gets recurring attention.

Priority order
1. auth/paywall
2. tone generation
3. blog publish/share
4. app sync
5. analytics
6. admin/ops

What each section should answer
- auth/paywall: Who pays, what is gated, and where is the source of truth?
- tone generation: Can the library still expand, and are at least 2 variants produced when touched?
- blog publish/share: Does blog content reach the intended surfaces?
- app sync: Is the iOS surface receiving the right content without shared accounts?
- analytics: Are the signals still useful for decisions?
- admin/ops: Can the platform be maintained without manual guesswork?
