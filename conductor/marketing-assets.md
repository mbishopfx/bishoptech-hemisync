# Cognistration Marketing Assets

This document defines the safe, clear, and premium marketing direction for Cognistration.

Primary marketing goal
- Make the product feel calm, premium, trustworthy, and easy to understand.
- Keep the language clear and non-hypey.
- Focus on user experience, ritual, consistency, and audio craftsmanship.
- Avoid medical promises, government/intelligence claims, and trademark risk.

Brand voice
- Clear
- Calm
- Premium
- Confident, but not exaggerated
- Helpful and human
- Precise without sounding clinical or deceptive

What we should say
- Focus sessions
- Calm downshifting
- Better routines
- Attention support
- Rest and recovery rituals
- Guided audio experiences
- Premium listening sessions
- Clean, simple, high-trust design

What we should avoid
- Medical claims like cure, treat, heal, diagnose, or replace care
- Claims that imply guaranteed results
- References to Hemi-Sync or other restricted trademarks
- Government, military, or intelligence storytelling as a core sales hook
- Overly technical jargon unless it directly helps the reader understand the product

Product positioning
- Cognistration is a premium audio experience platform for focus, rest, recovery, and intentional resets.
- The website should explain the value quickly and simply.
- The iOS app should feel like a calm companion with a strong paywall and a clean upgrade path.
- Blog posts should support trust, clarity, and discovery, not sensationalism.

Approved messaging pillars
1. Calm focus
   - For getting into a steady work state
   - For reducing friction before deep work

2. Rest and recovery
   - For winding down at the end of a long day
   - For building a consistent reset ritual

3. Premium audio craft
   - Clean signal design
   - Thoughtful layering
   - High-quality listening experience

4. Simple daily ritual
   - Easy to start
   - Easy to repeat
   - Easy to upgrade

SEO / blog direction
- Write around user intent, not sensational claims.
- Favor topics like:
  - how to build a better focus routine
  - what a calm audio reset can do for your day
  - how to create a nightly wind-down ritual
  - how premium audio experiences support consistency
  - how to choose a focus session for your workflow
- Blog posts should read like thoughtful product education and in-depth editorial writing, not conspiracy or medical copy.
- Ghostwritten pieces should sound human, specific, and mechanically grounded, with real logic behind each claim.

Tone rules for blog generation
- Use short, readable paragraphs.
- Lead with the user problem, then the product value.
- Keep every article grounded in what the app actually does.
- If a claim sounds like a health promise, soften it into experience-based language.
- If a claim sounds like a guarantee, remove it.

Landing page direction
- Headline: clear, calm, and specific
- Subhead: explain the benefit in one sentence
- CTA: simple and direct
- Avoid mystery language and overworked metaphors

Suggested headline patterns
- Find your focus, then keep it.
- Calm audio for work, rest, and reset.
- Premium sessions for a steadier day.
- A quieter way to get back on track.

Suggested blog categories
- Focus and productivity
- Evening reset and recovery
- Audio design and craft
- Ritual and habit building
- Product updates and feature education

Safe disclaimer
- Cognistration offers audio experiences for focus, rest, and routine support. It is not medical advice and does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition.

Implementation notes
- Use this file as the source of truth for blog post generation prompts.
- Use the same voice across landing pages, email, app copy, and social posts.
- If a blog draft sounds sensational, rewrite it to be clearer and calmer.
- If a draft leans on the old HemiSync framing, replace it with Cognistration positioning.

Example blog prompt template
- Write a clear, premium blog post about how a calm audio reset can support a steadier workday.
- Keep the tone grounded and trustworthy.
- Avoid medical language, hype, and government references.
- Tie the article back to the Cognistration experience and the value of a repeatable ritual.
